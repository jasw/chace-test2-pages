Study flow

  1. Start with core function language: domain, range, graphs, composition, inverse.
  2. Move to transformations because every later graph relies on them.
  3. Then do rational, odd/even, inequalities, and modulus as further functions.
  4. Then exponential functions and solving methods.
  5. Finish with logarithms, since they reverse exponentials and are central for solving.
